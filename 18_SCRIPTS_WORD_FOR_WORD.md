# 14 Ready-to-Film Scripts
## Word-for-Word — Just Hit Record

---

## How to Use These Scripts

1. Read the script out loud once before filming
2. You don't have to memorize it perfectly
3. Film 2-3 takes, pick the best one
4. The text overlays are written for you — add them in editing

---

## SCRIPT 1: DM Reactions
**Duration:** 8-12 seconds | **Cosplay Needed:** No | **Pillar:** Humor

### Setup
Screen record 3-4 interesting/weird/funny DMs you've received. You'll film yourself reacting to each one.

### What You Say
```
[Looking at phone, reading first DM]
"Okay so... let me show you what my DMs look like"

[Show DM #1 on screen]
[Your reaction — could be confusion, laughter, shock]

[Show DM #2 on screen]
"Wait... what does that even mean?"

[Show DM #3 on screen]
[Biggest reaction — this is the punchline]
"Y'all are unhinged. Never change."
```

### Text Overlays
```
0:00 — "POV: My DMs after posting cosplay"
```

### Caption
```
My DMs are a strange place

What's the weirdest DM you've ever gotten?

#cosplay #cosplayer #cosplaygirl #cosplayhumor
```

## SCRIPT 4: Cost Breakdown
**Duration:** 17-22 seconds | **Cosplay Needed:** Yes (laid out in pieces) | **Pillar:** BTS

### Setup
Lay out every piece of one cosplay on a bed/table. Have price amounts ready. Film each piece, then the total reveal.

### Shot Order
```
SHOT 1: You in the finished cosplay, looking good
[Text: "What people think this cost: $500"]

SHOT 2: Transition to reality — pieces laid out

SHOT 3: The wig
[Say: "Wig: $25 on Amazon"]
[Text: "Wig: $25"]

SHOT 4: The main outfit piece
[Say: "The [top/dress/etc]: $15, thrift store"]
[Text: "Top: $15"]

SHOT 5: Props/accessories
[Say: "Props: I made these. $12 in foam and paint"]
[Text: "Props: $12"]

SHOT 6: Contacts/makeup
[Say: "Contacts were like $20, makeup I already had"]
[Text: "Contacts: $20"]

SHOT 7: Running total appears
[Text: "TOTAL: $72"]

SHOT 8: Back to you in the full cosplay
[Say: "Looking expensive on a budget"]
```

### Caption
```
What this cosplay actually cost

The secret is I'm just broke with good taste

Want to see more of these?

#cosplay #cosplayer #cosplaytips #cosplaybudget #cosplaygirl
```

## SCRIPT 5: Transformation


## SCRIPT 7: Trend Audio
**Duration:** 5-10 seconds | **Cosplay Needed:** Yes | **Pillar:** Trend

### Setup
Find a trending audio that fits cosplay. Add your own twist.

### How to Find Trending Audio
```
1. Go to Reels, scroll for 5 minutes
2. Note any audio you see 3+ times
3. Check if it fits cosplay context
4. Save it for later
```

### General Formula
```
[Trending audio plays]
[You doing the trend BUT in cosplay]
[Add physical comedy or unexpected twist]
```

### Caption
```

#cosplay #[charactername] #[trendname] #cosplayer
```

## SCRIPT 14: Personal/Real Talk
**Duration:** 10-15 seconds | **Cosplay Needed:** Optional | **Pillar:** Personal

### Setup
Front camera. Natural lighting. Just you being honest.

### What You Say (Choose Your Topic)
```
OPTION A — Why you cosplay:
"People ask why I put all this effort into dressing up for a weekend.
And honestly? It's the one place where being extra is the point.
Where everyone gets it. Where you can just... be."

OPTION B — What it means to you:
"This started as a fun thing.
Now it's... the thing that got me through [hard time].
The community that showed up for me.
So yeah. It's more than costumes."

OPTION C — Confidence journey:
"First time I wore a cosplay in public I was terrified.
Now? I don't care who stares.
This is me. Take it or leave it."
```

### Text Overlays
```
None needed — let the words land
OR simple text like "why I cosplay"
```

### Caption
```
Just some thoughts

Thank you for being here

#cosplay #cosplayer #cosplaycommunity #realltalk
```
