Case Study: Two Videos
Why one reel got 1.9M plays and another got 889
THE PATTERN

The 5-Point Viral Checklist

Every reel that broke out passes all 5. Every underperformer misses most of them.

1
Specific Hook
Numbers, names, or a concrete setup — not vibes. The viewer knows what this reel is about in under 2 seconds.
2
Something HAPPENS
A story arc with a beginning, middle, and end. Tension, surprise, or payoff. Not just a mood — an event.
3
Original Audio
Your voice, your narration, your personality. 5.4x play multiplier over licensed tracks. The algorithm rewards creators, not curators.
4
Specific Caption with Numbers
"$187" not "a lot." "4 times" not "so many." Specificity triggers curiosity and makes the reel feel like a real story, not a thesis statement.
5
10-20 Second Sweet Spot
Long enough to tell a story, short enough to replay. 20-30s reels avg 200K plays. Under 10s avg 13K. Brevity with substance wins.
