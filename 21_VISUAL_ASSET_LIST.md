## QUICK REFERENCE — Shot Types

### Talking Head
- Eye level
- Head + shoulders in frame
- Room at top for text
- Clean background

### Full Body
- Phone 6-8 feet back
- Full body + some headroom
- Good lighting on costume
- Consider background

### Detail
- Close-up
- Use back camera for quality
- Steady (tripod or prop phone)
- Fill frame with the detail

### POV
- From your perspective
- Usually back camera
- Slightly shaky = authentic
- Capture the environment

### Time-lapse
- Locked position (tripod)
- Consistent lighting
- Capture full process
- Speed up 4-8x in editing
